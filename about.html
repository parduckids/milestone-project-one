About Us

We offer the best surf experience!

A SURF TRIP YOU NEVER FORGET

Our goal is to offer genuine and unforgettable surf vacations in Australia. We made a conscious choice to keep everything as small as possible from the beginning—smaller groups, smaller teacher-to-student ratios, and a smaller crew. This, in our opinion, enables us to provide a more individualised surf experience, allowing for greater flexibility and customization of our guests' demands.

Join us! Explore our stunning coastline and its various surf spots for a few hours while taking in Australian culture and, most importantly, catching some spectacular waves. During your stay with us, it is our first aim to ensure that your surfing improves, and our committed and energetic team makes this happen!

MEET THE TEAM

We are a small, friendly, and passionate team of surfers who came together to provide unique and memorable surf holidays in Australia. Wavy Wake was founded in 2014 by one local from Marmite (Joshua), and one British traveler (Sidney), who fell in love with the surf culture and region. Since then, we have expanded our team a little to include two more surf instructors - Alice and Chris.
